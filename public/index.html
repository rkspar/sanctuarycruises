The Sanctuary
&
Captain
Otho Campbell

Motor Vessel Sanctuary is a Corinthian 40 Catamaran Commercially built for the dive charter business and modified with kitchen, dining table, grill, sound system bathroom and all the toys required for a party on the water. Sanctuary is coast guard certified and inspected for 39 passengers and 2 crew. She can operate in all the shallows of Biscayne bay and sandbars or pull right up on the beaches for island parties. Captain otho Campbell has skippered over 700 parties on the bay and knows every gunk hole, getaway, sight to see and party spot from Haulover to Stiltsville.
